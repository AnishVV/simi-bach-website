GETTING AROUND

No rental car needed. CDMX is incredibly easy to navigate and Uber is reliable, safe, and cheap — you'll use it constantly.

  • Uber: safe, affordable, works great in CDMX
  • Metro for longer rides (we'll guide you!)
  • Both neighborhoods are very walkable
  • No need to rent a car

WHAT TO PACK

CDMX in July is warm and occasionally rainy. Pack light, breathable layers and bring your going-out energy.

  • Light, breathable outfits (it's warm!)
  • Comfortable walking shoes — you will walk
  • Swimsuit + cover-up
  • Sunscreen, bug spray, lip balm
  • Power adapter (Type A/B — same as US)
  • Portable charger
  • At least one nice dinner look

  • Travel insurance is strongly recommended
