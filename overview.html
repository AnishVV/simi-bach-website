☀  WHY WE'RE GOING

FOR OUR GIRL

Simi is the kind of person who makes every room feel warmer, every night feel longer, and every memory feel worth keeping. Before she says "I do," we're taking her to one of the most electric cities in the world — because she deserves nothing less.

Mexico City was made for this. The food, the art, the color, the energy — it's a city that celebrates life at full volume. We'll eat well, drink better, dance until we forget what day it is, and come home with stories we'll be telling for decades.

☀  OUR GROUP ENERGY

HER PEOPLE

We've known each other through every chapter — late nights, big moves, hard years, and the best nights of our lives. This crew shows up, shows out, and knows exactly how to celebrate the people they love.

Expect good conversations over mezcal, spontaneous dance floors, long dinners that turn into something more, and the kind of belly laughs that still hurt the next morning. We're Simi's people — and this weekend is ours too.

Good friends. great moments. unforgettable memories.

☀  WEEKEND HIGHLIGHTS

WHAT'S INCLUDED

STAY

A curated villa in the heart of Roma Norte — arched windows, courtyard mornings, and a rooftop that feels made for golden hour.

EAT + DRINK

Curated reservations at CDMX's most beloved spots. Shared plates, smoky mezcal, and a table full of people you love.

EXPLORE

Flower markets at dawn, world-class murals, cobblestone streets, and museum corridors. CDMX reveals itself to people who wander.

CELEBRATE

Dancing until the city sleeps, pool afternoons, rooftop toasts, and a few surprises we've been planning for months.
