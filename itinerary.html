The Plan

Day
by Day

Four days in the most electric city in Latin America. Markets and mezcal, rooftops and ruins, tacos and toast-worthy moments — here's everything we've planned for our girl.

¡salud a ella!

DAY 1

Wed July 30 · Arrival + Welcome Dinner

Bienvenidas!

Touch down, check in, and let CDMX wrap you up in its warm chaos. Tonight we gather for the first time as a group — welcome cocktails, a long table dinner, golden hour views, and the first of many toasts to our girl.

Where Roma Norte Rooftop
Vibes Warm welcome + good energy
Includes Welcome dinner + cocktails
DAY 2

Thu July 31 · Markets, Museums + Wandering

Explore

CDMX on foot. We wind through the grand plazas of Centro Histórico, duck into markets overflowing with color, and wander Condesa's tree-lined streets at whatever pace feels right. No rush — just the city doing its thing.

Where Centro Histórico + Condesa
Vibes Culture, color + curiosity
Includes Museums, markets + local time
DAY 3

Fri Aug 1 · Tacos, Mezcal + Rooftop Night Out

Eat + Drink

The big night. We eat our way through Roma and Juárez — from the city's best taco counter to a mezcal tasting that'll ruin you for bad spirits forever. Then we move the party up to the rooftop and dance until the skyline tells us to stop.

Where Roma + Juárez
Vibes Bold flavors + late nights
Includes Tacos, mezcal tasting + drinks
DAY 4

Sat Aug 2 · Brunch, Shopping + Goodbyes

Hasta Luego

One last morning together. We take it slow over a long brunch, do a final sweep through Polanco's boutiques and Roma's shops for anything we missed, and hug it out until the next time. CDMX, you were everything.

Where Polanco + Roma
Vibes Grateful hearts + full bellies
Includes Brunch + shopping
