Don't miss these

Saved
Spots

  • Café Nin

    Matcha, mood lighting, and the right people — a moment disguised as a coffee order.

  • Panadería Rosetta

    Guava rolls that sell out by 9am. Set an alarm. Worth every early morning.

  • Quintonil

    If budget allows — reservation essential. Chef Jorge Vallejo's finest indigenous cuisine.

  • Salón Malafama

    Mezcalería with great vibes — dimly lit, cumbia-adjacent, zero attitude.

  • Mercado Roma

    Fresh market bites, mezcal flights, and the best people-watching in Roma.

Local Tip

Slow Down
& Soak
It In

Mexico City rewards the unhurried. The best moments here happen between the plans — the street taco you didn't know you needed, the cantina you ducked into out of curiosity, the morning coffee that turned into a two-hour conversation.

Resist the urge to optimize every hour. The city will give you more than you could ever schedule.
